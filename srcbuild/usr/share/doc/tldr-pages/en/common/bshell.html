bshell

A GUI for browsing for SSH/VNC servers on the local network. See also: bssh and bvnc. More information: https://manned.org/bshell.

  • Browse for both SSH and VNC servers:

bshell

  • Browse for SSH servers only:

bshell --ssh

  • Browse for VNC servers only:

bshell --vnc

  • Browse for both SSH and VNC servers in a specified domain:

bshell --domain {{domain}}
