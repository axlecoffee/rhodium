msgunfmt

Decompile message catalog from the binary format. More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/gettext/manual/html_node/msgunfmt-Invocation.html.

  • Output conversion:

msgunfmt {{path/to/file.mo}}

  • Convert a .mo file to a .po file:

msgunfmt {{path/to/file.mo}} > {{path/to/file.po}}
