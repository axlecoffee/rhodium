rss2email

Deliver news from RSS feeds to an email program. More information: https://github.com/rss2email/rss2email.

  • List all feeds:

r2e list

  • Convert RSS entries to email:

r2e run

  • Add a feed:

r2e add {{feed_address}}

  • Add a feed with a specific email address:

r2e add {{feed_address}} {{new_email@example.com}}

  • Delete a specific feed:

r2e delete {{number_of_feed_in_list}}

  • Display help:

r2e {{[-h|--help]}}
