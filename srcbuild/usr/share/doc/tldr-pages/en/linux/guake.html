guake

A drop-down terminal for GNOME. More information: https://github.com/Guake/guake.

  • Toggle Guake visibility:

<F12>

  • Toggle fullscreen mode:

<F11>

  • Open a new tab:

<Ctrl Shift t>

  • Close the terminal:

<Super x>

  • Go to the previous tab:

<Ctrl PageUp>

  • Search the selected text in the browser:

<Ctrl Shift l>
