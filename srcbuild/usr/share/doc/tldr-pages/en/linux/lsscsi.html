lsscsi

List SCSI devices (or hosts) and their attributes. More information: https://manned.org/lsscsi.

  • List all SCSI devices:

lsscsi

  • List all SCSI devices with detailed attributes:

lsscsi {{[-L|--list]}}

  • List all SCSI devices with human-readable disk capacity:

lsscsi {{[-s|--size]}}
