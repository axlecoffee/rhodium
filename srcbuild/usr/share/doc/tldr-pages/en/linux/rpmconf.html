rpmconf

Handle RPMNEW, RPMSAVE and RPMORIG files left over by package upgrades. See also: rpm. More information: https://github.com/xsuchy/rpmconf.

  • List leftover files and interactively choose what to do with each of them:

sudo rpmconf --all

  • Delete orphaned RPMNEW and RPMSAVE files:

sudo rpmconf --all --clean
