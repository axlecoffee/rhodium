yakuake

A drop-down terminal for KDE. More information: https://manned.org/yakuake.

  • Start up Yakuake:

yakuake

  • Toggle Yakuake visibility:

<F12>

  • Toggle fullscreen mode:

<F11>

  • Open a new tab:

<Ctrl Shift t>

  • Switch between tabs:

<Shift {{ArrowLeft|ArrowRight}}>

  • Create terminal splits:

<Ctrl {{)|(}}>

  • Switch between splits:

{{<Ctrl Tab>|<Ctrl Shift Tab>}}
